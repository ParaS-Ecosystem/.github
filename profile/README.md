## 🚀 Introduction

The **ParaS Ecosystem** is a next-generation, **open-source software platform** based on the **SYCL programming model**, providing a **unified** and **hardware-agnostic programming framework**.  

It enables developers and researchers to **write applications once in SYCL** and **run them seamlessly across CPUs, GPUs, and emerging accelerators**, achieving both **high performance** and **portability**.  

The ecosystem integrates a comprehensive set of components — including the **ParaS Compiler**, **optimized mathematical and communication libraries**, and essential **developer tools** such as **debuggers** and **profilers** — offering a **complete environment** for development, deployment, and execution of **High-Performance Computing (HPC)** and **AI workloads**.  



---
## 🔭 Vision

> **Code Once, Execute on All**

**ParaS** empowers the **global open-source community** to develop, optimize, and scale **scientific and AI applications** across diverse hardware platforms.  

By reducing dependence on **vendor-specific solutions** and simplifying **development complexity**, the ecosystem promotes **open collaboration** and **innovation**.  

Its **open and extensible architecture** positions ParaS as a **sustainable software foundation** for **next-generation Exascale computing and beyond**.

## ⚙️ Components

### 🔹 [ParaS Applications](https://github.com/CDAC-SSDG/ParaS-Ecosystem/tree/main/ParaS-Application)
Built on the ParaS ecosystem, these reference and domain-specific applications demonstrate scalable performance and portability for scientific, engineering, and AI workloads across heterogeneous platforms.

### 🔹 [CLAP library](CLAP)
CLAP provides unified and optimized BLAS, LAPACK, FFT, and PDE solvers for CPUs, GPUs, and accelerators within a single scalable framework.

### 🔹 [ParaS Compiler](ParaS-Compiler)
The ParaS Compiler, compliant with SYCL 2020, enables “Code Once, Execute on All” programming by abstracting hardware complexity and ensuring long-term portability.

### 🔹 [ParaS Tools](https://github.com/CDAC-SSDG/ParaS-Ecosystem/tree/main/ParaS-Tools)
ParaS offers debuggers, profilers, and performance analyzers that enhance application visibility, correctness, and optimization on heterogeneous platforms
